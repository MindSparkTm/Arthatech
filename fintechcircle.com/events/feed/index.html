The FINTECH Circle Institute in association with Thomson Reuters is excited to announce a new series of FINTECH Master Classes focused on:

  • Regulatory Technology (RegTECH) – 7 November 2017
  • Fintech Innovation across Investment Management (WealthTECH) – 5 December 2017
  • Innovation across Insurance (InsurTECH) – 31 January 2018

The speakers are experienced thought leaders who work on the most cutting edge innovation and technology solutions and will bring to live how the financial technology innovation will impact all areas of finance.

RegTECH MasterClass

Global Overview on the “Fintech Revolution” with focus on Regulatory Change
Innovation across Regulatory Technology, Compliance & Risk Management.

RegTECH Platforms
Enterprise Innovation inspired by RegTECH Solutions
The Future of Regulation, Compliance & Risk Management
The courses are limited to 25-30 attendees to ensure active discussion & collaboration opportunities both among attendees and the hand picked RegTECH companies who will present case studies.

Speakers include:

Stuart Martin – Managing Director, Risk Information & Learning Services at Thomson Reuters providing an overview from a Risk, Regulation and RegTECH perspective

Susanne Chishti – CEO of FINTECH Circle & the FINTECH Circle Institute, Co-Editor of the Bestseller “The FINTECH Book” leading a global community of 75,000 fintech entrepreneurs, investors, financial services professionals globally providing cutting edge insights on latest RegTECH developments & trends

Wendy Jephson – Co-Founder & Chief Behavioural Scientist at Sybenetix (sold to Nasdaq mid 2017), Expert in how to systematically improve performance, risk and conduct management at the individual, team and company-wide level. Wendy will share her insights on behaviour management tools needed to improve decision-making skill, counter behavioural biases and poor conduct.

Peter Lancos – CEO Exate Technologies. As Data and GDPR expert Peter will address how to protect sensitive data in order to allow for greater client trust with firms and to comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) including explanation of the latest tools such as Sovereign Data Walls.

Patrick Butler – CEO, Calitor. As expert in Governance, Risk and Compliance in Financial Services, Patrick will provide an update on the latest developments across regulatory sandboxes globally and the regulatory perspective to RegTECH innovation.

Places are limited and by invitation only. Volume discount is available when booking 2 or 3 classes either for RegTECH or in combination with the InsurTECH and WealthTECH Seminars. Looking forward to seeing you soon.
